<div class="section">

<h2>Project Overview</h2>

<p>
This project focuses on the engineering design and planning of a 
<strong>5 Ton Per Hour (TPH) semi-automatic batching cattle and poultry feed plant.</strong>
The design includes process flow development, plant layout planning, 
equipment selection, and material handling system design.
</p>

</div>

<div class="section">

<h2>Project Objectives</h2>

<ul>

<li>Design a 5 TPH feed manufacturing plant</li>
<li>Develop process flow diagram (PFD)</li>
<li>Create industrial plant layout</li>
<li>Select machinery and equipment</li>
<li>Perform material flow analysis</li>
<li>Develop 3D models using SolidWorks</li>

</ul>

</div>

<div class="section">

<h2>Feed Manufacturing Process</h2>

<p>
The feed manufacturing process includes multiple stages to convert raw materials 
into nutritionally balanced animal feed.
</p>

<ul>

<li>Raw Material Intake</li>
<li>Weighing Hopper</li>
<li>Bucket Elevator</li>
<li>Magnet Separator</li>
<li>Hammer Mill (Grinding)</li>
<li>Feed Mixer</li>
<li>Pellet Mill</li>
<li>Cooler</li>
<li>Crumbler</li>
<li>Screener</li>
<li>Packing</li>

</ul>

</div>

<div class="section">

<h2>Equipment Used</h2>

<ul>

<li>Hammer Mill – 5 TPH</li>
<li>Ribbon Mixer – 1 Ton per Batch</li>
<li>Pellet Mill – 5 TPH</li>
<li>Counter Flow Cooler</li>
<li>Bucket Elevator</li>
<li>Screw Conveyor</li>

</ul>

</div>

<div class="section">

<h2>Plant Capacity</h2>

<ul>

<li>Production Capacity: 5 Ton Per Hour</li>
<li>Batch Size: 1 Ton</li>
<li>Batches per Hour: 5</li>
<li>Operating Time: 8 – 16 Hours</li>

</ul>

</div>

<div class="section">

<h2>Power Requirement</h2>

<ul>

<li>Hammer Mill – 75 HP</li>
<li>Pellet Mill – 150 HP</li>
<li>Mixer – 20 HP</li>
<li>Cooler – 10 HP</li>
<li>Conveyors – 15 HP</li>

</ul>

<p><strong>Total Power Requirement: ~270 HP</strong></p>

</div>

<div class="section">

<h2>Project Cost Estimate</h2>

<li>Electrical Installation: ₹10,00,000</li>

</ul>

<p><strong>Total Estimated Cost: ₹1.25 Crore</strong></p>

</div>
